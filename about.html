About Us

Wigwam was founded in 2003 by Richard "Ricky" Brown.
Recognising the importance of sustainability within landscaping, the team take a holistic approach to building gardens that benefit not only the people who enjoy them but the wider ecosystem as a whole.

The UK's biodiversity is one of the most threatened in the world. Since WWII, it has seen a 95% decline in wildflower meadows due to rapid urbanisation and climate change. This is just one example that threatens the existence of our native wildlife.

Wigwam are commited to designing and building gardens that have nature and wildlife in mind.

Since its conception, Wigwam has won numerous prestigious awards.
Most recently, the '2022 RHS Gold Award for Sustainable Design'.
